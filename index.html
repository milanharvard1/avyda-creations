Our Story

Stitched with ancestral love

Our parents were born in Mexico and built a home in Madison, WI filled with warmth, color, and tradition. At the heart of that tradition was our grandmother Theresa — a master of arts and crafts who could turn any material into something beautiful and meaningful.

Her passion passed down through generations and found us — here, with our hooks, weaving frog energy and fae magic into every piece. Avyda Creations is her legacy, made new.
