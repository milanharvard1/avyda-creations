Our Story

For Grandma Theresa

Our parents were born in Mexico and built a life in Madison, WI full of color, warmth, and tradition. At the heart of that tradition was our grandmother Theresa — a master of arts and crafts who could make something beautiful out of anything she touched.

“She taught us that every stitch is a conversation with the people who came before us.”

That passion passed down through generations and landed here — in our hands, in our hooks, in Avyda Creations. We blend the dark magic of Irish fae mythology with the bold, joyful colors of our Latina heritage. Every piece is both worlds at once.

Latina-owned, born & crafting in Madison, WI
Grandmother Theresa's love in every stitch
Dark fae aesthetics + vibrant Latina color energy
100% handmade — no machines, ever
